What is the difference between fundamental and technical analysis? ▼
Fundamental analysis examines company financials (revenue, earnings, debt) to determine intrinsic value, while technical analysis studies price charts and patterns to predict future movements. StockIQ combines both approaches for comprehensive stock evaluation.
How do I interpret RSI and MACD indicators? ▼
