Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: December 2024

30-Day Money-Back Guarantee: We offer a full refund within 30 days of your initial subscription for new customers who are not satisfied with our service.

1. Refund Eligibility

You are eligible for a full refund if:

  • You request a refund within 30 days of your first subscription

  • Your account is in good standing

2. Subscription Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time:

  • Log into your account dashboard
  • Go to "Account Settings" → "Subscription"
  • Click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Confirm your cancellation

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period. You will retain access until then.

3. Prorated Refunds

For subscriptions beyond the initial 30-day period:

  • Monthly subscriptions: No prorated refunds for partial months
  • Annual subscriptions: Prorated refunds available for unused months
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Subscription renewals after 30 days of initial purchase
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations
  • Services already consumed or accessed extensively
  • Third-party fees or charges

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  • Email us at support@stockiq.tech
  • Include your account email and reason for refund
  • Provide your subscription details
  • We will respond within 24-48 hours

6. Refund Processing

Approved refunds will be processed:

  • Back to your original payment method
  • Within 5-7 business days of approval
  • You will receive email confirmation
  • Bank processing may take additional 3-5 days

7. Subscription Downgrades

Instead of cancellation, you may:

  • Downgrade to a lower-tier plan
  • Changes take effect at next billing cycle
  • No refunds for plan differences
  • Contact support for assistance

8. Free Trial Policy

StockIQ offers a 3-day free trial with the following terms:

9. Dispute Resolution

If you're unsatisfied with our refund decision:
