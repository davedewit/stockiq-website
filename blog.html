Introducing AI Stock Chat - Your Personal Investment Assistant

March 8, 2026 • By Dave De Wit

We're excited to announce our newest feature: AI Stock Chat - an intelligent assistant that helps you navigate StockIQ's powerful analysis tools and answers your investment questions in real-time.

What Can AI Chat Do?

  • Direct Stock Analysis - Ask "Should I buy AAPL?" and get an instant clickable link to analyze Apple stock
  • Feature Navigation - "How do I use the S&P 500 screener?" - AI guides you to the right tools
  • Market Insights - Get quick answers about our 3,469 stock pages, crypto universe, and international screeners
  • Personalized Help - AI knows if you're logged in and adjusts responses for your account status

How to Use It

Look for the blue chat button in the bottom-right corner of any page. Click it to start chatting! Your conversation history persists as you navigate between pages, so you can ask follow-up questions seamlessly.

Smart & Context-Aware

The AI knows about all StockIQ features including:

  • 13 market screeners (S&P 500, NASDAQ 100, international markets)
  • Crypto universe analysis
  • Dashboard favorites and CSV exports
  • Pricing plans and free trial details

Try It Now!

The AI chat is live on all pages. Try asking questions like:

  • "Analyze TSLA"
  • "How does your crypto screener work?"
  • "Compare AAPL vs MSFT"
  • "What's included in the free trial?"

We're continuously improving the AI's knowledge and capabilities. Have feedback? Let us know through the chat!
